A Message from the Dean
The COVID-19 pandemic is unprecedented. The College of Sciences and the AUM community is prepared to assist students in achieving their academic goals. Faculty and staff are prepared to transition to distance learning. For questions regarding a course, please contact your professors. For assistance with advising and registration, contact central advising and/or your department chair. Email addresses and office phones are here http://www.aum.edu/people-finder.
